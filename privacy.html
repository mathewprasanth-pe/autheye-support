Overview

AuthEye is designed with a privacy-first approach. This app analyzes images to determine authenticity (e.g., real vs AI-generated) while ensuring that your data remains on your device.

Data Collection

AuthEye does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Image Processing

All images selected by the user are processed locally on the device. No images are uploaded to external servers, stored, or shared.

Permissions

AuthEye may request access to:

  • Camera – to capture images for analysis
  • Photo Library – to select images from your device

These permissions are used solely for functionality. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

AuthEye does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally, your data never leaves your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Children’s Privacy

AuthEye does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe to use by all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
